Reportify Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the AI-Powered Student Report Generation System (Reportify); A2Z release. The app is designed to assist teachers in generating well-written detailed student reports using AI services provided by OpenAI and Google. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Teacher Information: We collect and permanently store the names of active teachers provided by the institute.
  • User Information: Teachers sign up for the app using their institute-provided email. We collect and permanently store this information.
  • Class and Student Information: Teachers upload class and student data from their grading matrices, including class details and student names and genders. This data is temporarily stored for three months, after which it is permanently deleted.
  • Report Information: We collect and permanently store generated reports, including the report content, associated student and class data, and teacher feedback.
  • Student Performance Input: We collect the teachers’ input about their students’ performance used to generate reports to ensure reports match the input.
  • Regeneration Information: When a report is regenerated, we collect and permanently store information about the old and new reports to improve our services.
  • Levels and Level Objectives Information: We collect and permanently store information about the institute’s levels and level marker objectives.

How We Use Your Information

  • Generate and improve student reports.
  • Monitor the quality of our services.
  • Identify and resolve issues with report generation.
  • Monitor app usage to prevent malicious actions.
  • Measure the success of the system.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain temporary data (class and student information) only for three months. This data is then permanently deleted. All other collected data, including reports and associated information, is stored permanently to support ongoing service improvement, monitoring, and success measurement.

Data Security Measures

We take the security of your information seriously. We use a combination of technical and administrative security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access and use.

User Rights and Control

Users have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of their personal information. For inquiries or requests related to your data, please contact us using the information provided below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
